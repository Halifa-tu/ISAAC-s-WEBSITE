FAQ

Common Questions

Do you offer remote support?
Yes! Hali-Tech provides full remote support for software issues, installations, troubleshooting, and more. All you need is an internet connection. Contact us via WhatsApp or call and we'll connect to your device securely.
Can you come to my home or office?
Absolutely. We offer home and office visits within Oyarifa and surrounding areas in Accra. Just book an appointment via WhatsApp or call and we'll arrange a time that works for you.
How much does a service cost?
Pricing depends on the type and complexity of the service. Our basic software services start from GH₵50. We offer transparent, upfront quotes with no hidden charges — contact us for a free estimate before committing to anything.
How long does a typical repair or setup take?
Most software services (Windows installation, virus removal, optimization) are completed within 1–3 hours. Website projects typically take 3–7 days depending on complexity. We always give you an honest timeline upfront.
Do you build websites for students and small businesses?
Yes! We specialize in business websites, personal portfolios, and school/project websites at student-friendly prices. Whether you need a single page or a multi-page site, we've got you covered.
Is my data safe when you access my computer remotely?
Yes. We only access your system with your full permission, and you can watch everything we do in real time. We use trusted, industry-standard remote support tools and never access your files beyond what is needed to fix the issue.
